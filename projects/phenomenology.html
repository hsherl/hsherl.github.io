Phenomenology in Medicine Reading Group

This reading group will explore mental disorder from a phenomenological perspective. All meetings will be on the KCL Strand Campus, in room 605 of the Philosophy Building, at 18:00-19:00 on Mondays.

1st meeting: February 10th.

We will read "Gilles Deleuze: psychiatry, subjectivity, and the passive synthesis of time" by Marc Roberts (Nursing Philosophy 7, no. 4 (2006): 191-204).

2nd meeting: February 17th.

We will hear a presentation on the use of literature in medicine and its relation to phenomenology.
