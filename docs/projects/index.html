A selection of recent projects:

  • At King’s College London:
    • I co-organized reading groups with the Philosophy Society about the philosophy of mental disorder (joint with the Psychiatry Society), the epistemology of peer disagreement, and the philosophy of Alexander Bogdanov.
    • I co-wrote the class materials for a graduate class in physics about Dark Matter and Dark Energy (7CCP4935).
    • I presented a cohort study by Brown et. al., on the use of the Mental Health Act (2005) in the South London and Maudsley Trust, for the Psychiatry Society’s journal club.
  • I have presented introductory philosophy talks for upper secondary school classes about the philosophy of science, epistemology of peer disagreement, philosophy of language, Wittgenstein, philosophy of mathematics, Pyrrhonian skepticism, and the philosophy of time travel.
  • I have refereed philosophy papers for Dialexicon Journal.
  • I have translated English psychology articles to Norwegian for Practical Health Psychology.

Do not hesitate to get in touch if you or someone you know is looking for help with similar projects. If I have time, I would love to help out.
